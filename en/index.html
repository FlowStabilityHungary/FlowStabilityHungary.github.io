Maarten Vanierschot

KU Leuven, associate professor

We work together with Prof. Maarten Vanierschot in the field of drag reduction techniques. We research together the effect of miniature vortex generators on the laminar-turbulent transition.

Péter Rucz

BME HIT, assistant professor

We investigate the role of flow oscillations in aeroacoustics with Dr. Péter Rucz. We developed models of musical instruments and other strongly coupled acoustic-aerodynamics systems, like cavity tone.
